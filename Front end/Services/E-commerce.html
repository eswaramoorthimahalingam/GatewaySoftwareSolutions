Ecommerce Development FAQs

Common questions about our ecommerce development services

How long does it take to build an ecommerce website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic store typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex enterprise solutions can take 8-12 weeks or more. We provide a detailed project timeline during the planning phase.

Which ecommerce platform is best for my business?

The best platform depends on your specific needs. Shopify is great for quick launches, WooCommerce offers more customization for WordPress users, and Magento is ideal for large enterprises. We'll recommend the best option during our consultation.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages that include security updates, performance optimization, bug fixes, and feature enhancements. All our development packages include post-launch support.

Can you migrate my existing store to a new platform?

Absolutely! We specialize in ecommerce migrations, ensuring a smooth transition of your products, customers, orders, and SEO rankings to the new platform with minimal downtime.
