Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our mobile app development services

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The timeline depends on the complexity of your app. A basic app can take 4-6 weeks, while more complex applications may require 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support packages that include bug fixes, updates, and feature enhancements. Our support duration varies based on the package you choose.

Can you help with app store submission?

Absolutely! We handle the complete app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing all necessary assets and documentation.

What is the difference between native and cross-platform apps?

Native apps are built specifically for one platform (iOS or Android) using platform-specific languages. Cross-platform apps use frameworks like React Native or Flutter to build once and deploy on multiple platforms, offering cost and time savings.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we take client confidentiality seriously. We're happy to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement before starting any project to protect your intellectual property and business ideas.
