AI Agent Development FAQs

Common questions about our AI agent development services

How long does it take to develop an AI agent?

The timeline depends on complexity. A basic conversational AI agent takes 4-8 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions with custom ML models can take 12-20 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during the discovery phase.

Do you need our data to train the AI models?

Yes, quality data is essential for training effective AI models. We work with your data while ensuring complete privacy and security. For specific use cases, we can also leverage pre-trained models and transfer learning.

Can AI agents integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating AI agents with existing CRM, ERP, databases, and other business systems through APIs and custom connectors. We ensure seamless integration with your current technology stack.

How do you measure AI agent performance?

We track multiple metrics including accuracy, response time, user satisfaction, task completion rate, and business KPIs. You'll receive detailed performance reports and continuous optimization based on real-world usage data.
