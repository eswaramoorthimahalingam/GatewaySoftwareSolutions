Our Services

What development services does Gateway offer?

Gateway provides comprehensive digital solutions including Custom Web Applications, Mobile Apps (iOS & Android), E-commerce Platforms, UI/UX Design, Cloud Solutions, DevOps, and ongoing Maintenance & Support services tailored to your business needs.

Do you work with startups or only established businesses?

We work with businesses of all sizes! From startups needing MVPs to enterprises requiring complex systems, Gateway scales its services to match your stage, budget, and vision. We even offer special startup packages.

Can Gateway handle both design and development?

Absolutely! Our full-service approach includes UI/UX design, prototyping, development, testing, and deployment. We create beautiful, functional digital products from concept to launch.

Development Process

What is Gateway's development methodology?

We follow an agile, transparent process: Discovery & Strategy, Design & Prototyping, Development Sprints, Quality Assurance, Deployment, and Post-launch Support. You're involved at every stage with regular updates and demos.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites: 4-6 weeks, Web applications: 8-16 weeks, Complex mobile apps: 12-20 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during project planning.

How do you ensure project quality?

Quality is built into our process through code reviews, automated testing, manual QA, performance optimization, and security audits. We maintain 99%+ client satisfaction through rigorous quality standards.

Pricing & Payments

What are Gateway's pricing models?

We offer flexible pricing: Fixed Price (defined scope), Time & Material (agile projects), and Dedicated Team (long-term partnerships). We'll recommend the best model during our consultation.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer milestone-based payments for larger projects, monthly retainers for ongoing work, and flexible payment terms to accommodate your budget and cash flow requirements.

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs. We provide transparent quotes with detailed breakdowns. Any third-party costs (hosting, APIs, etc.) are discussed and approved upfront before being incurred.

Technology Stack

What technologies does Gateway specialize in?

We're experts in modern technologies: Frontend (React, Vue, Angular), Backend (Node.js, Python, .NET), Mobile (React Native, Flutter), Cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), and emerging technologies like AI/ML integration.

Can you work with our existing technology stack?

Absolutely! We're technology-agnostic and can work with your current stack or recommend the best technologies for your specific project goals, scalability needs, and budget constraints.

How do you ensure application security?

Security is paramount. We implement industry best practices: secure coding standards, regular security audits, penetration testing, data encryption, and compliance with relevant security standards.
